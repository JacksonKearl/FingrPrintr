Data & Privacy

There are a couple cookies, one that maintains your name, one that caches your browser fingerprint. These are both necessary for the app to function, thus no cookie banner.

When you enter a room, your name is published to the room via the welcome message. This message, and any you send, are stored by Cloudflare and accessible to the world at large. The most recent 100 messages in each room are maintained indefinitely.

Deleting information is not available to the public at large, but can be requested by contacting me via email (see my GitHub).

Tracking

This site uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to track performance and usage. Users are not tracked at an individual level.
